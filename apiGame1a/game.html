High low card game.

2 cards will be dealt. 1 faceup, the other facedown.
Press HIGHER or LOWER to indicate if you think the value of the faceup card will be higher or lower than the facedown card.
Start off with a score of 100. It costs 5 points to play. Winning a single hand returns 8 points.
Winning hands can be rolled into later hands for a multiplier.

Example. Winning a first hand gets 8 points at a cost of 5. Netting the player 3 points.
Rolling that 8 points back in and winning again would win 16 points, while still only costing 5. Thus netting the player 11 points.
Currently both cards are shown at all times for this demo of the running code. In stylized complete version, the second card would be shown "facedown"
and have an animation "flipping" it when the player makes their high/low decision.
